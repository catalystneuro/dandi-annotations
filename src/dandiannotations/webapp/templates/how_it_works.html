How It Works

Understanding the DANDI External Resources submission and review process

Process Overview

The DANDI External Resources system uses a two-tiered approach to ensure quality and relevance of community contributions.

1. Submit

Community members submit external resources related to DANDI datasets through our web form

2. Review

Moderators review submissions for relevance, quality, and adherence to community standards

3. Endorse

Approved resources are endorsed and made available to the broader DANDI community

Detailed Process

Step 1: Community Submission

What happens during submission:
  • Contributors fill out a detailed form with resource information
  • System validates all required fields and formats
  • Submission is saved to the "community" folder for review
  • Contributors receive confirmation of successful submission
Required Information:
Resource Details:
  • DANDI Set ID
  • Resource name and URL
  • Repository information
  • Resource type and relation
Contributor Information:
  • Name and email (required)
  • ORCID identifier (optional)
  • Personal/institutional URL (optional)

Submissions are immediately visible in the "pending" section

Submit Resource

Step 2: Moderation Review

Review Criteria:
  • Relevance: Resource must be directly related to the specified DANDI set
  • Quality: Resource should provide value to the research community
  • Accessibility: Links must be functional and publicly accessible
  • Appropriateness: Content should align with DANDI community standards
Moderator Actions:
  • Review submission details and verify resource accessibility
  • Check that the resource URL is functional and appropriate
  • Assess relevance to the specified DANDI set
  • Make endorsement decision with moderator attribution

Trusted community moderators review all submissions

Moderation Interface

Step 3: Endorsement

What happens during endorsement:
  • Moderator provides their contact information for attribution
  • Resource is moved from "community" to "endorsed" status
  • Endorsement date and moderator information are recorded
  • Resource becomes visible in the official endorsed section
Benefits of Endorsement:
  • Credibility: Community trust through moderator review
  • Quality Assurance: Verified relevance and accessibility
  • Discoverability: Prominent placement in endorsed section
  • Provenance: Full attribution trail maintained

Endorsed resources receive community seal of approval
